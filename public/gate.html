GATE Exam Pattern

Overview

The Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering (GATE) is an examination that primarily tests the comprehensive understanding of various undergraduate subjects in engineering and science. GATE is conducted jointly by the Indian Institute of Science and seven Indian Institutes of Technology.

Exam Structure

  • Mode of Examination: Online (Computer Based Test)
  • Duration: 3 hours
  • Total Marks: 100
  • Total Questions: 65
  • Question Types: Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs) and Numerical Answer Type (NAT) Questions

Question Pattern

Type of Question Number of Questions Marks per Question
MCQs 30 1
MCQs 35 2
NAT 20 1
NAT 10 2

Subjects Covered

GATE covers a wide range of subjects in engineering and science. Here are some of the major subjects:

  • Aerospace Engineering
  • Civil Engineering
  • Computer Science and Information Technology
  • Electrical Engineering
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Physics
  • Chemistry
