Introduction to Graphic Design

Graphic design is the art and practice of planning and projecting ideas and experiences with visual and textual content. It is a way of communicating through visual elements.

Basic Principles of Graphic Design

Balance

Balance in design is the distribution of the visual weight of elements. It can be symmetrical or asymmetrical.

Contrast

Contrast is used to emphasize the difference between elements, making one stand out more.

Alignment

Alignment creates a sharper, more ordered design by ensuring elements are placed with a purpose.

Repetition

Repetition strengthens a design by tying together otherwise separate parts and creating associations.

Proximity

Proximity helps to create relationships between elements by grouping related items together.
