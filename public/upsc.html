Overview

The Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) conducts the Civil Services Examination (CSE) to select candidates for various civil services of the Government of India, including IAS, IPS, IFS, and others.

Eligibility

Candidates must be Indian citizens, meet age and educational qualifications, and adhere to the specified number of attempts.

Syllabus

The syllabus includes topics from General Studies, Essay, and optional subjects. Detailed syllabus can be found on the official UPSC website.

Exam Pattern

The exam consists of three stages: Preliminary, Mains, and Interview. Each stage is designed to test the candidates' knowledge, analytical skills, and suitability for a career in civil services.

Important Dates

Application start date, exam dates, and result announcements are periodically updated on the UPSC website.
